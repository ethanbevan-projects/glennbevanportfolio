Client: Barefoot Power

Project: Home LED Lighting Kit with Solar Panel and Battery

Project Success Criteria

  • Replacing kerosene lamps with environmentally friendly solar powered LED lighting.
  • Tackling a major health issue regarding lung disease and indoor air quality.
  • Targeting off-grid communities in Africa and India to tackle energy poverty and improve health.
  • Designed a range of battery packs and lighting solutions for households.
  • Included USB charging for phones.
  • Designed a commercial system for schools and institutions.
  • Low cost solution targeted towards poor communities – micro financed for affordability.
  • Profit making products for social good.
  • Distributed widely in Africa and India.
