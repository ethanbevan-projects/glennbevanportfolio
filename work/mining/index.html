Mining and Construction Products

This category shows a sample range of products from the multi-year ongoing partnership with Leica Geosystems and Topcon. The products that were developed generally have highly sensitive GPS and GNSS location and tracking systems housed in ruggedised enclosures with touchscreen tablet interfaces. These devices are used for machine guidance in mining operations, surveying and commercial agriculture attached to machinery such dump trucks, dozers and tractors. The key features of these devices is their ability to withstand harsh environments with IP rated enclosures, incorporating vibration resistance, thermal management, and EMC considerations.
