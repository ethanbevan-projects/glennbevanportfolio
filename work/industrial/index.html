Industrial Products

The Industrial Products category is a mixture of building and construction products, access control systems, minesite remote management and solar panel control systems. The Enviromesh non- corrosive concrete reinforcement product won multiple awards in 2010 including the Australian International Design Award, Powerhouse Museum Award, award for Excellence in Sustainability and a finalist in the World Index Awards. The Passive Fire System Fire Collar also won a Gold Good Design Award in 2016
